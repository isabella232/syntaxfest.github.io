TLT 2019 - Paris - August 28-29

The 18th International Workshop on Treebanks and Linguistic Theories will take place, as one of the 4 events of the Syntax Fest 2019.

It will occur on the last week of August (Aug 26-30), in the center of Paris, in the "Grand amphitheatre du Monde anglophone" of the Sorbonne Nouvelle.

Important Dates

  • Submission deadines: April 28, 2019 for lo ng papers and June 5, 2019 for short papers.
  • Acceptance notification: TBA
  • Camera-ready version: TBA
  • TLT 2019: August 28-29 (within Syntax Fest, Aug. 26-30)

    Reviewing process

    On the submission page, authors submit their paper only once for the whole SyntaxFest, composed of 4 conferences, but they can uncheck conferences they do not wish their paper to be considered for. If the paper is deemed appropriate for more than one of the selected conferences, the SyntaxFest joint organization committee decides on the final placement of the paper, which implies the day of the presentation and the proceedings the paper will appear in.
